Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

+

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is available for 2-3 business days delivery, and overnight shipping is available in select areas.

What's your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unopened products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, simply contact us within 30 days for a full refund.

Do you offer warranty on products?

+

Yes! All our products come with manufacturer's warranty. We also offer extended warranty options that you can add during checkout.

Do you offer repair services?

+

Yes, we provide professional repair services for iPhones. Contact our support team to schedule a repair appointment at our service center.

Can I track my order?

+

Absolutely! You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. You can use it to track your package in real-time.

Do you price match?

+

Yes, we offer competitive pricing. If you find a lower price elsewhere for the same product, contact us and we'll match it plus offer an additional 5% discount.
